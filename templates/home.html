1. Can we take legal actions against a friend who doesn't return our money? +

You can file a summary civil suit, a criminal breach of trust case, or send a legal notice. You may also recover the debt under the Indian Bankruptcy Code or Negotiable Instruments Act.

2. Is it legal to kill someone in self-defence in India? +

Yes, the right to private defence allows this under extreme circumstances, as outlined under Sections 96–106 of the IPC.

3. How long does the Supreme Court take to complete litigation? +

Admission hearing occurs in about 15 days. After admission, the second hearing is usually after 3 months. Detailed final hearings (if required) can take up to 3 years.
